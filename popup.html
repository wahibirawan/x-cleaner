Quick Guide
To delete your posts
1
Open your X Profile → tap the Posts tab.
2
Select Delete mode in the toggle above.
3
Turn on Undo Reposts if you also want to remove reposts.
To remove your likes
1
Open your X Profile → tap the Likes tab.
2
Select Unlike mode in the toggle above.
⚙
Set Batch Size and Cooldown to control speed and avoid X's rate limit. Then tap Start Cleaning.
